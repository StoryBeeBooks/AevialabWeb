Account & Data Privacy

Where is my data stored?

Aevia Revive was built with a "privacy-first, on-device" architecture. Your logs, photos, and insights are generated and stored entirely on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your data is securely synced to your personal Apple CloudKit account. We do not maintain remote access to your health data.

Do you use AI or send my photos to the cloud?

No. In our version 1.0 architecture, we removed all reliance on cloud-based LLMs or vision processing APIs. All data aggregation, pattern analysis, and allergen detection run deterministically on your local device.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account by navigating to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action revokes your Sign in with Apple credential and completely wipes your local SwiftData store. Because we don't store your logs on our servers, there are no backups for us to purge — your data (including all household profiles on your device) is deleted instantly.

Logging & Nutrition Data

Where does the nutritional data come from?

Aevia Revive uses heavily curated, offline bundles composed primarily of:

  • Canadian Nutrient File (CNF 2015): The primary offline database.
  • USDA FoodData Central: For specific supplement exceptions.
  • Published Scientific Databases: Various international food databases (e.g., Japan MEXT, Australian FSANZ) to provide insight into exotic whole foods and phytonutrients.
Why did my meal return an "Unavailable" value for certain nutrients?

If an ingredient requires nutritional parsing that falls outside of our curated, on-device tier systems, it may return unavailable rather than hallucinating or guessing a value. Try using the exact name of the whole food.

Can I log a completely custom food?

Currently, Aevia Revive operates off a strict semantic catalog to ensure high-fidelity insights and phytonutrient tracking. If you can't find an exact item, we recommend logging its primary raw constituents.

Allergen Scanner & Warnings

How does the Allergen Scanner work?

The on-device Allergen Scanner uses deterministic text-matching against the FDA Big-9 allergens (milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, soy, sesame).

Is the Allergen Scanner 100% accurate?

No. The scanner is a supportive tool, not a medical or safety device. It checks the text titles of foods in your plate. It cannot detect cross-contamination, mislabeled foods, or unlisted ingredients in processed foods. Always verify the safety of your food independently if you have severe allergies.

Insights & Patterns

What are "Patterns"?

In the Dashboard, the Patterns card computes plain-language signals by comparing your current eating window to your previous window. It checks for metric consistency, macronutrient trends, and dietary diversity (e.g., scoring you against the American Gut Project metric of 30+ distinct plant foods per week).

Does Aevia Revive provide medical advice?

No. All pattern insights, synergy breakdowns, and micronutrient comparisons (such as tracking against FDA Daily Values) are strictly for educational reference. Consult your physician or a registered dietitian before undergoing significant dietary shifts or starting a fasting protocol.

Household Profiles & Subscriptions

Can I share Aevia Revive with my family?

Yes. Aevia Revive features a multi-profile household system. The app opens to a Profile Gate where you can select who is logging data. Each profile's logging history and insights are completely isolated from others.

Is there a limit to how many profiles I can create?

Your app comes with 1 primary owner profile. You can unlock up to 6 additional seats (for a total of 7 profiles) via our Household subscriptions. Each additional seat is managed natively through your Apple ID settings.
