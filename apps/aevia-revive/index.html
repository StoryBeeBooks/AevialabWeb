01 — Vision-Powered Telemetry

Photograph your plate. Decode its biology.

Aevia Revive eliminates manual entry. Simply photograph your raw ingredients or a prepared meal and the app's visual AI engine estimates portion sizes and weights with precision. That data is instantly cross-referenced with the Canadian Nutrient File — the country's authoritative dietary database — delivering real-time macro and micronutrient readouts calibrated to your actual inputs.

  • AI portion estimation from a single photo — no scales required.
  • Canadian Nutrient File as the accuracy benchmark for all nutrient data.
  • Immediate readouts for macros, micros, and caloric density.
  • Functions purely as a factual calculator — no dietary directives.

02 — Bioactive Profiling

Beyond calories. Into molecular intelligence.

Where conventional nutrition apps stop at macros, Aevia Revive maps your food to a proprietary bioactive compound database. Logging an ingredient reveals its precise molecular profile — illustrating, for example, how the anthocyanins in a given berry modulate oxidative stress pathways, or how sulforaphane from cruciferous vegetables activates Nrf2-mediated antioxidant defenses.

  • Proprietary database of phytochemicals, polyphenols, and bioactive metabolites.
  • Real-time readout of daily phytochemical saturation by compound class.
  • Pathway mapping: see which cellular mechanisms each bioactive influences.
  • Objective data only — no supplement recommendations pushed.

03 — Metabolic Timing

Visualize your cellular metabolic state.

The app's user-controlled insight toggles track the biological timing of your meals and fasting windows. By visualising your cellular states — from insulin baseline through glycogen depletion, fat oxidation, and into autophagy — and aligning meal times with your local circadian rhythm, Aevia Revive frames daily fasting as metabolic conditioning rather than restriction.

  • Real-time metabolic state timeline: insulin, fat-oxidation, autophagy windows.
  • Circadian alignment engine calibrated to your time zone and sunrise/sunset.
  • Opt-in alerts — e.g., a reminder to stop eating three hours before sleep.
  • All alerts are user-defined and based entirely on your inputted schedule.
Screen · Metabolic Timing UI
04 — Exposome & Hydration

Monitor what surrounds you — inside and out.

Cellular health does not exist in isolation. Aevia Revive tracks both your internal electrolyte gradient and the external environmental stressors your body is navigating. Sodium, potassium, and magnesium inputs provide factual updates on cellular conductivity and hydration status. Simultaneously, real-time UV index and air quality data from your city context environmental oxidative load — and opt-in alerts allow you to adapt your protocol accordingly.

  • Electrolyte tracking: sodium, potassium, magnesium — cellular conductivity readout.
  • Live environmental data: UV index and air quality index by location.
  • Opt-in oxidative stress alerts tied to environmental thresholds.
  • Suggested protocol adjustments (e.g. antioxidant dosage) are fully user-controlled.

05 — Absolute Data Sovereignty

Your biology stays on your device.

Aevia Revive operates strictly as a wellness and telemetry tool. All photographic records, nutritional logs, metabolic timelines, and bioactive readouts are stored entirely on your local device. No health data is transmitted to external servers. Your biological information remains completely private and under your absolute control — by architectural design, not policy promise.

Local-First Architecture
All data processing and storage occurs on-device. No cloud sync, no remote health databases.
No Personal Health Transmission
Photographic records, meal logs, fasting windows, and bioactive data never leave your device.
Pure Analytics Engine
Aevia Revive provides data — it does not prescribe, diagnose, or make medical recommendations.
Opt-In Everything
Alerts, location access, and environmental monitoring require explicit user activation and can be disabled at any time.
