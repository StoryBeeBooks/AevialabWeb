Accessibility Statement

Aevia Lab
Last Updated: May 1, 2026

1. Our Commitment

Aevia Lab is committed to making aevialab.com usable for the widest possible audience, including people who rely on assistive technologies. We aim to conform with WCAG 2.1 Level AA where reasonably achievable.

2. What We Do

  • Use semantic HTML structure with descriptive headings, landmarks and link text
  • Maintain colour contrast ratios that meet WCAG 2.1 AA for body text
  • Provide alt text or aria-hidden treatment for decorative imagery
  • Support full keyboard navigation across the Site
  • Use a responsive layout that scales from 320 px viewports upward
  • Avoid auto-playing audio and seizure-inducing motion

3. Known Limitations

The Site is under active development. Some decorative SVG illustrations and gradient panels may not provide meaningful information when read by a screen reader; these are marked aria-hidden where appropriate.

4. Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on the Site, or if you require content in an alternative format, please contact us at research@aevialab.com and describe the issue. We will respond and make a reasonable effort to provide the information you need.
